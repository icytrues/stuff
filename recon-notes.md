### ASN lookup 

#### amass 

###### Find ASNs 
`amass intel -org OrgName`

###### Find domains on ASN
`amass intel -active -asn 123,456,789`

#### ipinfo.io
`curl http://ipinfo.io/127.0.0.1`

#### ASNLookup.com 
`curl http://asnlookup.com/api/lookup?org=OrgName`
